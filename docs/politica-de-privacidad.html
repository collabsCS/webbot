C&S Collabs - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2025

At C&S-Collabs, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and how we protect it.

1. Data Collected
RandomHunter only collects the following technical and anonymous data:

  • Total number of servers where the bot is active
  • Most used languages
  • Most used commands
No personal or identifiable user data is collected (e.g., usernames, emails, Discord IDs, or sensitive information).

2. Purpose
The collected data is strictly used to:
  • Analyze bot usage
  • Improve functionality
  • Identify community language preferences
We do not sell, share, or use the data for commercial purposes.

3. Third-Party Services
If you choose to donate, payments are processed through external platforms such as Ko-fi. C&S-Collabs does not collect or access any financial or personal information submitted through those services.

4. Security
We follow best practices to protect the data we collect and prevent unauthorized access. Since the data is technical and anonymous, associated risks are minimal.

5. Cancellation and Suspension
If the bot is suspended, shut down, or removed from your server, all previously collected anonymous data (e.g., command usage or language stats) may still be retained for statistical and development purposes only. No personally identifiable information is ever collected or stored.

6. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, you can contact us at: contact@cs-collabs.com
